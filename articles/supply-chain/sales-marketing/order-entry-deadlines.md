<span data-ttu-id="51eeb-110">Os prazos finais de entrada de ordem são usados como a entrada da promessa da ordem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51eeb-110">Order entry deadlines are used as input for order promising.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51eeb-111">Consequentemente, ajudam a gerenciar as expectativas do cliente sobre entregas de ordem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51eeb-111">Therefore, they help you manage customer expectations about order deliveries.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51eeb-112">Por exemplo, os clientes podem ver se fazem um pedido antes de um horário específico, você confirmará a entrega das mercadorias no mesmo dia.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51eeb-112">For example, customers can see that, if they place an order with you before a specific time, you will commit to shipping the goods on the same day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51eeb-113">Entretanto, se eles perderem esse prazo, podem esperar a remessa apenas no próximo dia útil.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51eeb-113">However, if they miss that deadline, they can expect the shipment only on the next business day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51eeb-114">Você define os prazos finais da entrada de ordem baseados em suas capacidades de depósito e agenda da transportadora.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51eeb-114">You set order entry deadlines based on your warehouse capabilities and shipping carrier schedules.</span></span>
