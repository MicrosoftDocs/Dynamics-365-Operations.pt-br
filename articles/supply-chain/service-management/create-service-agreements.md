## <a name="create-a-service-agreement-from-service-management"></a><span data-ttu-id="aa43e-105">Criar um contrato de serviço em Gerenciamento de serviços</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aa43e-105">Create a service agreement from Service management</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="aa43e-106">Navegue até **Gerenciamento de serviço**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aa43e-106">Navigate to **Service management**.</span></span>
