1. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-106">Vá para Gerenciamento e contabilidade de projeto > Projetos > Todos os projetos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-106">Go to Project management and accounting > Projects > All projects.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-107">Na lista, clique no link na linha selecionada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-107">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-109">Clique em Tarefa de item.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-109">Click Item task.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-110">Clique em Ordem de compra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-110">Click Purchase order.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-111">No campo Conta de fornecedor, insira ou selecione um valor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-111">In the Vendor account field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-114">No campo Depósito, insira ou selecione um valor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-114">In the Warehouse field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-115">Clique em OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-115">Click OK.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-116">Na lista, marque a linha selecionada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-116">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-117">No campo Número do item, insira ou selecione um valor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-117">In the Item number field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="1c029-118">Pode ser o número de item ou uma categoria de compras.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-118">This can be the item number or a procurement category.</span></span>

13. <span data-ttu-id="1c029-120">Clique na guia Projeto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-120">Click the Project tab.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="1c029-121">Verifique se as vendas e os preços de custo estão disponíveis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-121">Verify that the sales and cost prices are available.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1c029-122">Se não estiverem disponíveis, mas forem necessárias, insira as informações.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1c029-122">If they are not available but needed, enter the information.</span></span>
