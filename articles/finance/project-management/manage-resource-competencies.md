## <a name="set-up-competencies-certificates"></a><span data-ttu-id="84942-112">Configurar competências: certificados</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="84942-112">Set up competencies: Certificates</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="84942-125">Selecione **Salvar** e feche a página.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="84942-125">Select **Save**, and then close the page.</span></span>
