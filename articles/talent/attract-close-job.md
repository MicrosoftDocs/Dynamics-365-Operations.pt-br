## <a name="cancel-a-job"></a><span data-ttu-id="4a721-107">Cancelar um trabalho</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4a721-107">Cancel a job</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4a721-108">É possível cancelar um trabalho no Attract a qualquer momento.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4a721-108">You can cancel a job in Attract at any time.</span></span>

   ![Fechar trabalhos no Attract](./media/attract-close-job.png)

## <a name="close-a-job-as-position-filled"></a><span data-ttu-id="4a721-114">Fechar um trabalho como Cargo preenchido</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4a721-114">Close a job as Position filled</span></span>
