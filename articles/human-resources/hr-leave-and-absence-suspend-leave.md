## <a name="suspend-leave-and-absence-for-an-employee"></a><span data-ttu-id="599fb-105">Suspender licenças e ausências de um funcionário</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="599fb-105">Suspend leave and absence for an employee</span></span>
